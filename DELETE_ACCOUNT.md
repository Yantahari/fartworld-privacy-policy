# How to Delete Your FartWorld Account

## Request Account Deletion

To delete your FartWorld account and all associated data, please send an email to:

**fartworld.app@gmail.com**

Include the following information:
- Subject: "Delete my FartWorld account"
- The email address associated with your account

## What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon request, we will delete:
- Your user profile (username, avatar, category)
- Your fart recordings and scores
- Your team membership data
- Your achievements and statistics

## Processing Time

Account deletion requests will be processed within **30 days** of receiving your request.

## Data Retention

After account deletion:
- All personal data will be permanently removed
- Anonymous aggregated statistics may be retained
- Team scores may be retained without personal identification

## Contact

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at:
**fartworld.app@gmail.com**
